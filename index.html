About PhysicsLab

PhysicsLab provides accurate, interactive simulations of fundamental physics experiments. Our virtual laboratory allows students and enthusiasts to explore physics concepts without the constraints of a physical lab.

All simulations are based on real physics equations and provide real-time data collection for analysis. The platform is designed to complement traditional physics education.
